# Raw structures of PfPMT cluster models

- `3uj7holospherev1.pdb`: from [SI data](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/suppl/10.1021/acs.jpclett.9b01555/suppl_file/jz9b01555_si_002.zip) of [1].
- `cluster-ref.pdb`: residue-level selection of QM region from `3uj7holospherev1.pdb` according to the [SI document](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/suppl/10.1021/acs.jpclett.9b01555/suppl_file/jz9b01555_si_001.pdf) of [1].
- `cluster-capped.pdb`: H-capped `cluster-ref.pdb` with PyMol.
- `cluster-capped.xyz`: XYZ version of `cluster-capped.pdb`.

[1] Yang, Z.; Liu, F.; Steeves, A. H.; Kulik, H. J. Quantum Mechanical Description of Electrostatics Provides a Unified Picture of Catalytic Action Across Methyltransferases. J. Phys. Chem. Lett. 2019, 10 (13), 3779–3787. https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jpclett.9b01555.
